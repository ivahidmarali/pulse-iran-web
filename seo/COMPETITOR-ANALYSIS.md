# Competitor Analysis — پالس ایران (palsiran.com)

**Date:** June 2026  
**Business type:** Persian-language news aggregator (Publisher)  
**Target market:** Iranian diaspora + Persian-speaking global audience

---

## Competitive Landscape Overview

The Persian news aggregation space is dominated by two tiers:
- **Tier 1:** State-affiliated news agencies (IRNA, Tasnim, Mehr) — DA 73–80, massive institutional SEO
- **Tier 2:** Independent aggregators/portals — the real competitive set for palsiran.com

Palsiran.com competes in Tier 2 and has a structural differentiator no competitor offers: **transparent political bias labeling across 11 categories**.

---

## Primary Competitors (Tier 2 Aggregators)

### 1. khabarfarsi.com — خبر فارسی
**Type:** Automated news aggregator / search engine  
**Positioning:** "Smart news search engine — no human intervention in collection"  
**Estimated Monthly Traffic:** 500K–1M visits (estimated)  
**Primary traffic source:** Organic search  
**Audience:** Mixed — diaspora + Iran  
**Domain Authority:** ~55 (estimated)  

**Strengths:**
- Long-standing brand (10+ years)
- High keyword volume for branded term "خبر فارسی"
- Full-text search across aggregated news

**Weaknesses:**
- No political bias labeling
- Outdated UX
- No financial data integration
- No source credibility signals

**Our edge over them:** Superior UX, political transparency, live prices, structured categories

---

### 2. gooya.com (news.gooya.com) — گویا نیوز
**Type:** Persian news portal + directory  
**Positioning:** Oldest Persian news portal on the internet (est. ~1995)  
**Global Rank:** ~25,000  
**Primary traffic source:** Direct (77.85%) — brand loyal audience  
**Audience:** 72% male, dominant age 55–64, primary geo: USA  
**Domain Authority:** ~65 (estimated)  

**Strengths:**
- Iconic brand among older diaspora
- Institutional trust built over 25+ years
- Huge backlink profile

**Weaknesses:**
- Severely dated design — poor mobile experience
- Audience is aging (55–64 dominant cohort)
- No political bias labeling
- No financial data
- Zero modern SEO practices (minimal structured data)

**Our edge over them:** Modern design, mobile-first, younger audience capture (25–45), political transparency

## Keyword Gap Analysis

### Palsiran.com owns these differentiating keyword clusters:
- `گرایش سیاسی رسانه` — no competitor targets this
- `منابع خبری معتبر` — partial coverage by competitors
- `اخبار دلار و طلا` — price pages can rank for these
- `نرخ دلار لحظه‌ای` — owned by price tracker sites; palsiran can rank

### Competitor-owned keywords palsiran can target:
- `اخبار فوری` / `خبر لحظه‌ای` — khabarfoori.com's core keywords
- `اخبار ایران امروز` — broad news keywords
- `اخبار سیاسی ایران` — currently owned by tabnak/fararu
- `قیمت دلار امروز` — high-volume commercial keyword; our /prices page can rank

### Untapped opportunities (no strong competitor):
- `اخبار ایران از منابع مختلف` — multi-source framing
- `رسانه‌های اصولگرا` / `رسانه‌های اصلاح‌طلب` — source category pages
- `بررسی گرایش رسانه‌ها` — bias analysis content
- `نرخ سکه امروز` + `نرخ یورو امروز` — long-tail price keywords

## Audience Opportunity

**The diaspora gap:** Gooya's audience is 55–64 years old. Khabarfoori serves European diaspora. No competitor is aggressively targeting the **25–45 year old Iranian diaspora** with a modern, mobile-first experience. That's palsiran.com's primary audience acquisition opportunity.

**Geographic concentration:** Belgium, USA, Sweden, Germany, Canada, UK, Netherlands are the top diaspora markets. Content and UX decisions should optimize for these users.
